Connecting DataReader/DataWriter to Topics

Currently connector tools on the palette must be used to connect a DataReader or DataWriter to a Topic. The steps to make a connection are

  1. Select the appropriate connector from the palette.
  2. Click on the DataReader/DataWriter.
  3. For a DataWriter click on its associated Topic, MultiTopic, or ContentFilteredTopic figure. For a DataReader click on its associated Topic figure.
